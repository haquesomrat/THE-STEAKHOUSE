The Bancroft is a modern American steakhouse and craft cocktail lounge . Edgy and
urban, The Bancroft an exciting spot to socialize after work and a striking setting for a
special night out. The service style is warm and attentive. The environment is dramatic
and sexy. We want our guests to have a dining out experience, not just dine out. The
Bancroft strives to entertain, to impress, and to be memorable. As a locally owned
restaurant group, the Webber family seeks to provide gathering places that are a source
of pride for the community.
